About Weeding Master

Weeding Master is an engaging lawn maintenance simulation that transforms everyday yard work into an exciting challenge. Take control of professional gardening equipment and showcase your landscaping expertise.

With each level bringing new challenges and objectives, you'll need to master both precision control and tactical planning to become the ultimate Weeding Master.

Game Features

Professional Tools

Access a variety of professional-grade landscaping tools and equipment.

Diverse Environments

Work in various garden types, from small yards to extensive landscapes.

Skill Progression

Unlock new abilities and tools as you improve your gardening expertise.

Challenge Mode

Test your skills in time-limited challenges and special missions.

Weather System

Adapt to different weather conditions that affect gameplay mechanics.

Achievement System

Earn badges and rewards for mastering different gardening techniques.

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Movement control
  • Space: Activate equipment
  • E: Switch tools
  • Esc: Pause menu

Pro Tips

  • Plan your mowing patterns for efficiency
  • Keep an eye on your equipment condition
  • Use the right tool for each task
  • Complete bonus objectives for extra rewards
